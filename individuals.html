Individuals

We all struggle at times. Often the struggle is with something which has been around for an extended period of time or feels as though it has arisen at various times throughout life. At other times it is something completely new that disturbs our present life. Counselling is relevant where there is a sense of struggle. Counselling is a type of talking therapy that can be used to treat emotional and psychological concerns.

I offer both in-person and online counselling.

I am experienced in speaking with people about a wide range of concerns brought to private counselling. The perspectives I offer are based on my clinical experience, my knowledge of theoretical approaches and what I experience works for you as an individual.

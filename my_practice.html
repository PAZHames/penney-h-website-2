Private Counselling and Psychotherapy, Alton

As a private therapy practice I offer counselling and therapy to individuals and couples. I am fully registered with the Association for Family Therapy (AFT) and United Kingdom Council for Psychotherapy (UKCP), a leading UK professional body. With over a decade of experience in the NHS and in private practice, I am able to offer you a thoughtful, engaged, helpful counselling both in-person (face to face) and online. My practice is built upon the most widely recognised and respected therapeutic approaches for a wide range of issues and concerns.

Whatever your age, race, colour, nationality, religious or political belief, sexual orientation, disability or gender identity I value the opportunity to work with you.

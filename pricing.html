The initial session lasts a little longer than subsequent sessions. You'll probably have lots to say about why you have come and what you hoppe to acheive, you may have questions about the approach I take, the outcome you might expect and my training and experience. And I'll want to let you know about the limits to confidentiality, what we'll do about cancellations, and explain how I store your notes. To give ourselves enough time to do this comfortably, initial sessions last 75 rather than 60 mins. After that sessions run for 60 mins. Prices are the same for in person and by zoom.
